Chicken Biryani Recipe

Description

Chicken Biryani is a fragrant rice dish made with aromatic basmati rice, spiced chicken, and herbs. A beloved South Asian delicacy, biryani is known for its layers of rice and meat cooked together, creating a flavorful, aromatic, and colorful dish perfect for special occasions.

  • 2 cups basmati rice
  • 500 g chicken, cut into pieces
  • 1 large onion, thinly sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 2 tablespoons ginger-garlic paste
  • 2 teaspoons garam masala
  • 1 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1 teaspoon red chili powder
  • Fresh coriander and mint leaves
  • 4 cups water
  • 3 tablespoons oil or ghee

Steps

  1. Wash and soak basmati rice for 30 minutes. Drain.
  2. Heat oil, fry onions until golden brown. Set aside half for garnish.
  3. Add ginger-garlic paste, tomatoes, yogurt, and spices. Cook until fragrant.
  4. Add chicken and cook until sealed and slightly browned.
  5. Layer rice over chicken, add coriander and mint leaves.
  6. Pour water, cover tightly, and cook on low heat until rice is fluffy and chicken is cooked through.
  7. Garnish with reserved fried onions. Serve hot with raita or salad.
